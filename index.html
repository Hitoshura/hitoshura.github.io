About Me

Hello! I'm Josh, and I'm an aspiring web developer currently working through FreeCodeCamp and Team Treehouse coursework. I originally studied Computer Engineering Technology, but the programming coursework I was assigned led me to pursue a career in web development. Making this portfolio page has taken me quite some time, but I'm rather proud of it! I've learned quite a bit from this project; how to use shadows to give text an outline (despite not using them in the long run), how to make backgrounds stay fixed as you scroll down the page, and I'm rather proud of that semi-transparent little gallery below! I've also picked up some design tips along the way!
